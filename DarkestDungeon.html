Darkest Dungeon

Release year: 2015

Publisher: Red Hook Studios

Genre: roguelike, strategy

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"Darkest Dungeon" is a challenging gothic roguelike turn-based RPG about the psychological stresses of adventuring. Developed by Red Hook Studios, the game is set in a dark and atmospheric world where players manage a roster of heroes battling their way through a series of dungeons beneath a decrepit mansion. Players must not only fight monsters but also contend with various stressors like fear, paranoia, and starvation, which affect their characters' mental states and can lead to significant in-game consequences. The game is noted for its striking art style, complex mechanics, and brutal difficulty, emphasizing strategy and careful management of resources and personnel.
