House Flipper

Release year: 2018

Publisher: Frozen District

Genre: simulation, indie

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"House Flipper" is a popular simulation video game that taps into the thrill of renovation and real estate management. Developed by Empyrean and released by PlayWay S.A., the game allows players to buy, renovate, and sell houses for profit. Players perform a range of tasks from simple cleaning and painting to complete overhauls involving demolition and rebuilding. The game offers a detailed, hands-on approach to home improvement and design, where each choice can affect the sale price. It also features a variety of tools and materials, making each project unique and challenging. "House Flipper" appeals to those who enjoy creativity and strategy, offering a satisfying visual and financial payoff as players transform dilapidated properties into beautiful homes.
